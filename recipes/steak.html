Carne Asada

Carne asada is classic style of beef that is essential to many mexican dishes, making it very versatile and useful to have in your skillset. Carne asada has the key trait of containing a large array of flavors that all work together for an amazing taste. The key to an amazing carne asada dish is the marinade. Lucky for you we have an amazing recipe for you.

Ingredients:

  • ¾ cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ½ cup red wine vinegar
  • ½ orange, sliced
  • ⅓ cup lime juice
  • ⅓ cup orange juice
  • ¼ cup Worcestershire sauce
  • ¼ bunch cilantro, stems cut off and leaves chopped
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon coarsely ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried Mexican oregano
  • 2 teaspoons salt

Preparation:

  1. Combine soy sauce, olive oil, vinegar, orange slices, lime juice, orange juice, Worcestershire sauce, cilantro, garlic, black pepper, cumin, chili powder, oregano, and salt in a large bowl and whisk to combine.
  2. To marinate the beef, place meat in a gallon-sized freezer bag or in the bottom of a shallow baking dish. Pour marinade on top of beef, close bag or cover dish, and marinate at least 1 hour before grilling.
  3. Once you have properly marinated your meat, you can cook it any way you please, but grilling is the most popular cooking method used.
  4. The final and most importatn step is to enjoy your amazing work of art and a taste of Mexico!
